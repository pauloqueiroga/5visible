How to play TIKITALA Online

2 players, each having 8 chips

The game starts with 2 chips from each player on the table

The first player to get 5 of their chips showing is the winner!

Each player takes turns playing one move at a time

The icon at the center changes to show whose turn it is, and if they should tap/click to pick up or to drop a chip

One tap/click selects which chip to pick up

Another tap/click selects where to drop the chip

Stacks cannot have more then 3 chips

Players cannot move the chip most recently moved by their opponent
