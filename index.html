TIKITALA RULES

A simple and fun tabletop game that requires strategic thinking and sharp memory.

Ground Rules

2 players

The winner is the first player who gets 5 of their chips showing on top!

Players cannot peek or look under the topmost chips

Max 3 chips per stack

Players cannot move the chip most recently moved by their opponent

Start by placing 2 chips from each player on the table

Each player takes turns playing one move at a time

Possible Moves (remember the ground rules!)

Add a new chip to the table, starting a new stack

Add a new chip to an existing stack

Move one chip from one stack to another
